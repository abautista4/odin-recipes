Chickpea Salad

This one's a really easy recipe that requires no cooking. I make this a lot when I have to meal prep. Add or remove any ingredients that you want! This is the way I usually will make it. Enjoy!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans of chickpeas
  • 1 medium sized cucumber
  • 1/2 cup of crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/2 finely chopped red onion
  • 1 sliced avocado
  • 1 1/2 cup of sliced cherry tomatoes
  • 3 tbsps of extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tbsps of lemon juice
  • 1/4 cup of chopped cilantro
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • pressed garlic clove

Instructions:

  1. Combine the dressing ingredients in bowl: 3 tbsps of olive oil, 3 tbsps of lemon juice, pressed garlic clove, pinch of salt, pinch of pepper. Whisk.
  2. Combine remaining chickpea salad ingredients in a salad bowl, add dressing to taste (we used all of it) and toss to coat.
  3. Enjoy!
